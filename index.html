Quick LaTeX Reference

Here are some basic LaTeX commands to get you started:

  • Subscript: x_2 produces x₂
  • Superscript: x^2 produces x²
  • Fractions: \frac{1}{2} produces ½
  • Greek letters: \alpha, \beta, \gamma produces α, β, γ
  • Square root: \sqrt{x} produces √x
